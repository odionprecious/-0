ABOUT US

Guarding Generational Wealth Since 1977

Nitadol Bank was founded in Zurich in 1977 by a consortium of European private bankers and Swiss gold merchants. From its origins as a bullion custody specialist, it has grown into one of the world's foremost institutions for private wealth management, digital asset custody, and cross-border financial services.

Our philosophy is simple: your wealth belongs to you. We exist to protect it, grow it, and pass it on — across borders, across generations, and across asset classes from traditional bullion to next-generation blockchain assets.

With offices in Zurich, London, Singapore, Dubai, and New York, Nitadol operates at the intersection of old-world banking discipline and modern financial technology.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open a Nitadol Private Vault account?

Click "Open Private Vault" on the homepage and complete our two-step KYC registration. You'll need a valid ID, proof of address, and source of funds documentation. Account activation typically takes 1-2 business days after verification.

What currencies and assets does Nitadol support?

We support EUR, CHF, USD, GBP, AED, SGD, and 140+ additional fiat currencies. For digital assets, we support Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), USDT, USDC, and a range of regulated tokenised assets. Physical gold, silver, and platinum bullion custody is also available.

Is my money safe at Nitadol Bank?

Your funds are held in fully segregated accounts and never used for proprietary trading. Digital assets are held in cold storage and insured up to €50M per client. Nitadol is regulated by FINMA (Switzerland) and the FCA (UK), providing the highest level of regulatory oversight.

What are the fees for international transfers?

Standard accounts: €8 flat fee per international wire. Gold Tier clients: zero transfer fees and zero FX spread. Intra-Nitadol transfers between account holders are always free regardless of tier.

How do I upgrade to Gold Tier?

Gold Tier is automatically granted to clients maintaining a minimum balance of €50,000 or AUM of €250,000 across all vault holdings. Contact your relationship manager or email nitadolb@gmail.com to discuss tier eligibility.

Can I access my vault 24/7?

Yes. Our digital vault platform is accessible 24/7 via web and mobile. Customer support is available around the clock for Gold Tier and above. Standard tier support operates Monday–Friday, 07:00–22:00 CET.

What happens if I forget my PIN or password?

For security, account recovery requires identity re-verification. Contact us at nitadolb@gmail.com with your registered email and Nitadol ID. A secure recovery link will be issued within 2 hours after identity confirmation.
